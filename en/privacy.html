1. INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

When you register for a Andy English Bot account, Andy English Bot collects personal information such as your username, email address, birth date, gender, spoken and studied languages and any other personal or preference information that you enter into the Service, as well as your profile picture. Andy English Bot will use your personal information for the following general purposes: (i) to provide you with Services; (ii) to respond to your questions and comments; (iii) to communicate with you via regular mail and email (or other electronic means) about the Service; for (iv) statistical purposes and (v) otherwise as described to you at the point of collection. When you use the Service, you may set up your personal profile, form relationships, send messages, perform searches and queries, enrol in events and courses, and transmit information through various channels. Andy English Bot stores and collects this information so that Andy English Bot can provide you with the Service and offer personalised features. Andy English Bot also receives and records information from your computer and browser, including your IP address, cookie information, software and hardware attributes, and the pages you request, whether or not you register for an account. Andy English Bot uses such information to customise the advertising and Content you see, fulfil your requests for products and services, improve our Service, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal purposes and for external clients such as advertisers. If you choose to use our invitation service to tell a friend about our site, we will ask you for your friend's email address. We will automatically send your friend an email inviting him or her to visit the site. Andy English Bot may attempt to send invitations more than once. Andy English Bot stores this information to send such emails, to register a friend connection if your invitation is accepted, and to track the success of our referral programme. Your friend may contact us at contact@andychatbot.com to request that we remove this information from our database.

Please note that if you enter payment details into the Service when purchasing a Paid Service (including any Premium Membership), Andy English Bot itself does not receive or store such payment information, as such information is received, stored and used by our payment service providers for the purpose of completing the purchase. You can opt out of email communication initiated by Andy English Bot including newsletters, email notifications and progress reports, in your Settings on the Site.

3. COOKIES

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a website's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies or notify you when a cookie is set. If you want to delete any cookies that are already on your computer, please refer to the instructions for your browser to locate the file or directory that stores cookies. If you reject all cookies, you may not be able to use some features of the Service.

You can find out more about how we use cookies on Andy English Bot here: www.Andy English Bot.com/cookies

Andy English Bot lets other entities that show advertisements or provide services on some of our web pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other entities' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, and not this policy. Advertisers or other entities do not have access to Andy English Bot's cookies.
